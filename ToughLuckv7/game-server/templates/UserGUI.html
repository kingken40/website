Welcome to Tough Luck!

Get ready to test your luck and skills in this exciting game!

How to Play:

  • Choose the number of moves you want to play.
  • Select the difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Decide if you want to allow duplicate numbers in the game.
  • Enter your moves based on the numbers generated by the game.
  • Score points for correct moves and try to avoid wrong ones!
